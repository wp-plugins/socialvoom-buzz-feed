Display the Socialvoom Buzz Feed on your posts. The buzz feed shows relevant real time status updates from Facebook, Identica, Friendfeed and Google Blog Search.

== Description ==

This WordPress plugin displays the [Socialvoom](http://socialvoom.com/) Buzz Feed within your posts. This plugin grabs your post category and uses that to determine what feed results to show. We use several major APIs to aggregate the data and render it onto the feed, such sources include: Facebook, Identica, Friendfeed and Google Blog Search.

**Best Results**
For best results, use multiple categories throughout your blog. You are able to position the feed above or below your posts from within the Buzz Feed menu located just under the Settings menu.

**Responsive Design**
In order to deliver you a truly seamless experience, we developed the buzz feed to be responsive. What this means is despite the width of your posts, our buzz feed will automatically adapt in size and fit your blog with no additional css or editing.

**API Connectivity**
Please note, the buzz feed using multiple API's from across the internet, we have compiled them into one single API known as the Socialvoom Buzz Feed API which sends http requests to our servers in order to deliver real time buzz updates to your blog. We take pride in the level of commitment we put into our API security.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `svbuzzfeed.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Go to the Buzz Feed settings menu to set your feed position within your posts to either the top or bottom of the content.
